MODES:

Ctr-Alt-Enter: Toggle between typing mode. 
**Only possible when not in drag selection mode.

Ctr-Alt-V: Toggle drag-selection mode.

Ctr-Alt-D (right click): Toggle drawing mode

Ctr-Alt-R: fill selected area with last char.

EDITOR:
Ctr-Alt-C: Clear the editor.
Ctr-Alt-S: Save. If there is a selection, then that selection only will be saved.
Ctr-Alt-D: Drawing mode. 

NAVIGATING THE DRAG SELECTOR:
This can be done either with a arrow keys to move the selected area,
or with clicks of the mosue.

To select/move the basepoint of the selection, either double 
click or press Enter in selection mode.
